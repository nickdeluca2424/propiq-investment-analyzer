PropIQ

Multi-source real-estate investment analyzer — value any property, score it across four strategies, and model the deal in seconds.
What makes this analysis strong
With your own API key, PropIQ runs live multi-source web research — pulling list prices, sold comps, AVM estimates, and rent comps from across the public web, then reconciling them into a single defensible value range with a confidence score and the sources used. That feeds a deterministic scoring engine (same inputs → same score, every time) across House Hack, Buy & Hold, BRRRR, and Fix & Flip, plus full FHA payment math and 10-year projections.
Runs entirely in your browser. Your API key stays on your device and is sent only to Anthropic for the research step.

Cheat Sheet

Every input, every score — at a glance
How Valuation Works
When you use AI auto-fill, Claude searches the web for the subject property and comparable sales, rentals, and AVM estimates. It adjusts comps for size, condition, beds/baths, and recency, then reconciles everything into a point estimate + range. The confidence score reflects how many quality comps it found and how tightly they agree. You always see the comps and sources, and you can override any number before scoring.
The Inputs

Current asking price. The slider models offers −20% to +10%.
